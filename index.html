About Our Project

We are a group of the school Gimnasio Campestre Reino Britanico which is formed by 4 students. The objective of this steam project is to show how to create an Arduino robot for a science and robotics project which is based on a prototype well known by children and is quite striking when it comes to doing their work.

The Purpose

We consider that there are 3 educational benefits of this project, like, Acquisition of technical and conceptual skills through the construction of the Arduino robot. This practical approach to learning will help to better understand the topics covered. Encourages creativity and innovative problem solving by using recycled materials and dealing with technical challenges during the building process. Provides new opportunities and options for future careers related to this area.  

The step by step

To build the Wall-E Arduino we must follow step by step using simple and easily available materials. Different components are used in the project, from electronics like Arduino, motors, sensors,etc. In addition to easily recyclable materials such as cardboard, paper and chopsticks.

Some materials used

*L298N Motor Module *DC motors (2 units) *Servo motor (for head) *HC-SR04 ultrasonic sensor (for "sight") *9V battery and battery connector *Breadboard and jumper cables *220 ohm resistance *LED (For the eyes) * Soldering iron and tin (optional, if a more secure connection is desired) * Screwdrivers * Scissors and pliers * Hot silicone gun * Cardboard, plastic or 3D printed parts for body structure * Wheels (can be recycled from toys) * Shafts and motor suppor
